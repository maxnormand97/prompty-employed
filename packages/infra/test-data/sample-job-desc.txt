Senior Software Engineer — Frontend Platform

Requirements:
- 5+ years of experience in software engineering
- Expert-level TypeScript and React
- Experience building and maintaining design systems or component libraries
- Strong understanding of web performance, accessibility (WCAG 2.1), and testing
- Experience with monorepo tooling (Nx, Turborepo, or similar)
- Experience with AWS or other cloud platforms is a plus

Nice to have:
- Experience with Storybook, Chromatic, or visual regression testing
- Contributions to open source projects
- Experience working in a high-growth product company

Salary: AU$160,000 – AU$200,000 + equity
